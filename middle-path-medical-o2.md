# The Middle Path: Medical Oxygen Without the Markup

Before committing to the welding route, there's an option many patients don't know about.

You can often get an oxygen prescription cheaply — through a walk-in clinic or a telehealth visit, which costs far less than a neurologist appointment. With that prescription in hand, you can buy **medical-grade** oxygen from the same industrial gas suppliers (Airgas, Linde) that sell welding oxygen, at comparable prices. You skip the expensive home-delivery supplier (DME) entirely, and you get the benefit of cylinders that are cleaned and inspected under FDA rules.

Many patients assume that "prescription" automatically means "expensive DME company." It doesn't have to. If getting a prescription is realistic for you, this middle path is simpler and avoids any cylinder-handling concerns entirely.

> **If you can get a prescription easily, this is the simpler path.** The welding oxygen route is for patients who can't get one, or who need oxygen now and can't wait.
